Project Overview

Water Damage Restoration
& Full Home Remodel

This proposal covers a complete restoration and remodel following water damage — replacing damaged flooring throughout, fully remodeling the kitchen and both bathrooms, refreshing the entire interior with new paint, and replacing doors and windows as specified. The result will be a fully restored, beautifully updated home ready for comfortable living or future sale.

All work is performed by Timberline Build Co (CSLB #1136140), a licensed general contractor based in San Clemente, CA, with an established network of trusted specialty subcontractors.
