01 — Project Overview
A Master Bath Worth Coming Home To

This project is a complete gut and rebuild of the master bathroom at 7 St Elizabeth — demo everything down to the studs and come back with a fully tiled walk-in shower, pocket door for the toilet room, updated vanity with double sinks, and all new plumbing, electrical, and finishes throughout.

All work is performed by Timberline Build Co and our trusted trade partners, with Justin Sherman on-site throughout the project.

Concept Render — Final finishes TBD per material selections
02 — Scope of Work
What's Included
  • Full demo and haul away of existing bathroom
  • Hot mop shower pan (waterproofing base)
  • Full tile installation — shower walls floor-to-ceiling (96" high), toilet room walls, and bathroom floor (tile selections per allowances)
  • New walk-in shower — 68"×34" footprint with built-in shampoo niche
  • Frameless or semi-frameless shower glass enclosure
  • All shower plumbing rough-in and finish (shower fixture per client selection)
  • Pocket door for toilet/shower room — frame, install, hardware
  • Relocate light switch and outlet (required for pocket door)
  • Remove existing soffit + drywall patch
  • Supply and set double sink vanity (per allowance); plumb both sinks
  • Supply and set bidet toilet (per allowance); rough-in electrical and plumbing
  • Electrical: 3 recessed lights in shower/toilet room, vent fan replacement, 2 above-mirror lights (per allowance), dedicated bidet circuit
  • Replace 2 sliding glass closet doors and track (per allowance)
  • Hang 2 client-supplied mirrors
  • Install client-supplied towel bar and toilet paper holder
  • Drywall patching and backer board as needed
03 — Exclusions
What's NOT Included
  • Paint — walls and ceiling not included; can be added as a change order
  • Permits — this project is proceeding without permits
  • Faucets / fixtures — client to supply; installation included
  • Shower fixture — client to supply; installation included
  • Mirrors (2) — client to supply; hanging included
  • Towel bar and toilet paper holder — client to supply; installation included
  • Furniture removal or storage
  • Any structural changes beyond soffit removal
