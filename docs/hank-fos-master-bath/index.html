01 — Project Overview
A Master Bath Built Right, From the Ground Up

This project is a complete gut-and-rebuild of the master bathroom at 7 St Elizabeth — everything comes out down to the studs, and we come back with a fully tiled walk-in shower with a built-in niche, new pocket door for the toilet room, updated double-sink vanity, and all new plumbing, electrical, and finishes throughout.

All work is performed by Timberline Build Co and our trusted trade partners, with Justin Sherman on-site throughout the project.

Concept Render — Tivoli Alba tile, frameless glass, double vanity
02 — Scope of Work
What's Included
  • Full demo of existing bathroom — all tile, fixtures, and finishes removed and hauled away
  • Hot mop shower pan — proper waterproofing base for the shower
  • Full tile installation — shower walls floor-to-ceiling (96" high) including toilet room walls, and bathroom floor — Tivoli Alba tile by Porcelain & Stone
  • Built-in shampoo niche in shower — tiled to match
  • Shower glass enclosure — 68"×34" semi-frameless (upgrade to full frameless available)
  • Shower plumbing rough-in and finish — valve, trim, and showerhead
  • All plumbing rough-in and finish — shower, 2 sinks, bidet
  • Set and plumb double sink vanity (client-supplied vanity and faucets)
  • Set client-supplied bidet toilet — rough-in and hookup included
  • Pocket door for toilet/shower room — frame, hang, and hardware
  • Relocate light switch and outlet as needed for pocket door
  • Remove existing soffit and patch drywall
  • Electrical — bidet dedicated circuit, new vent fan, 2 above-mirror vanity lights, 3 recessed lights in shower/toilet room
  • Replace 2 sliding glass closet doors and track
  • Hang 2 client-supplied mirrors
  • Install client-supplied towel bar and toilet paper holder
  • Drywall and cement backer board as needed
