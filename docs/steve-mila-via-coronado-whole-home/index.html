Overview

Your Vision. Our Expertise.

This proposal covers a complete whole-home remodel of your 1,450 sq ft residence at 28711 Via Coronado — including a full kitchen renovation, two bathroom remodels, new flooring throughout, window and door replacement, whole-house electrical updates, interior painting, new baseboards, and a roof replacement with fascia repair. Every detail has been considered to deliver a beautiful, lasting result.

Timberline Build Co is a licensed General Contractor based in Orange County. We self-perform and closely supervise all work, with trusted trade partners for specialty scopes. We protect your home, communicate throughout the process, and don't consider the job done until you're fully satisfied.
