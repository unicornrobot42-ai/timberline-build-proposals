Project Overview

What We're Building

This proposal covers a full bathroom remodel at 609 E Avenida San Juan, San Clemente, CA. The scope includes complete demolition of the existing bathroom, construction of a fully enclosed cave-style shower with no glass enclosure, comprehensive tile installation throughout, updated electrical with new recessed lighting and sconces, all new plumbing connections including shower valve and toilet relocation, vanity and medicine cabinet installation, a new exhaust fan, ceiling repaint, and door replacement. All finish materials — tile, vanity, medicine cabinet, countertop slab, sconce fixtures, and ceiling paint — are provided by the client. Timberline supplies all labor, rough materials, and installation expertise.
02
Scope of Work

What's Included

🔨
Full Demolition

Complete tear-out of existing bathroom finishes, fixtures, and flooring. Removal of partition wall between vanity and toilet.

🧱
Shower Wall Framing

Frame and build fully enclosed shower walls — cave-style design with no glass enclosure required.

🪵
Drywall & Substrate Prep

Patch and repair non-wet areas. Install cement board and RedGuard waterproofing membrane in all wet zones.

🔥
Hot Mop (Shower Pan)

Professional hot mop of shower pan for complete waterproof integrity before tile installation.

◼️
Tile Installation — All Areas

Labor for tile on all bathroom walls and floor. Client supplies all tile. Includes setting, grouting, and finishing.

🚿
Plumbing — Shower Valve & Drain

Install and set shower valve. Relocate toilet drain. Reconnect toilet and vanity plumbing.

💡
Electrical — 4 Recessed Can Lights

Supply and install four recessed lights with proper electrical runs and switches.

🕯️
Electrical — 2 Sconces

Wire and install two vanity sconces. Client supplies fixture. Includes junction boxes and proper wiring.

💨
Exhaust Vent Fan

Supply and install new exhaust fan with exterior venting. No existing fan in this bathroom.

🪞
Vanity & Medicine Cabinet

Install client-supplied vanity. Frame and install medicine cabinet over sink. Labor only.

🎨
Ceiling Repaint

Prep and paint bathroom ceiling. Client supplies paint.

🚪
Door & Casing Replacement

Install 28" RH 6-panel door with generic casing. Includes door, hardware, and casing installation.
