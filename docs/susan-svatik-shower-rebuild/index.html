Scope of Work

What's Included

Complete breakdown by phase. All labor included. Tile material is a separate pass-through at your exact cost.

1

Waterproofing

Hot Mop · Shower Pan Protection
  • Hot mop waterproofing applied to shower pan — the critical moisture barrier that prevents water infiltration and protects the structure underneath
  • Full inspection of drain connection and substrate before waterproofing is sealed
2

Plumbing

Shower Valve · Drain · Finish Trim
  • New shower valve — rough-in and final trim-out
  • New shower drain — rough-in and connection
  • Existing angle stops in good condition — no replacement needed
  • No vanity supply or drain work required
3

Tile & Backer

Hardie Backer · Shower Walls · Floor · ~116 SF
  • Install Hardie cement backer board on all shower walls — proper substrate for long-lasting tile
  • Full tile installation on three shower walls, floor to ceiling (96" height)
  • Tiled shower floor with proper slope to drain
  • Approximately 116 sq ft total coverage (walls + floor, including 20% waste factor)
  • Built-in shower niche — recessed, tiled, and sealed into the shower wall
  • Professional grout and sealing on all tile surfaces

Tile material is a separate pass-through — you select and pay exact cost, no markup. See Tile Selection below.

4

Vanity & Light Fixture

Customer-Supplied Vanity · Existing Electrical
  • Install customer-supplied vanity — secure, level, and connect
  • Plumb vanity sink (existing supply and drain in good condition)
  • Install light fixture using existing power supply — no rewiring needed
5

Drywall & Paint

Patch · Mud · Texture · Full Bathroom Paint
  • Drywall repair on all areas affected by demo — patch, mud, and match existing texture
  • Full bathroom paint — walls and ceiling, clean professional finish
  • Final cleanup and walkthrough

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

The questions homeowners ask us most before signing.

How does the tile material cost work?
Tile material is a pass-through — you select the tile you want, and we order it. You pay the exact material cost with zero markup from us. Once you've picked a tile, we'll confirm the cost and add it as a separate line item so there are no surprises.
What is the shower niche?
A built-in shower niche is a recessed shelf tiled directly into the shower wall — a clean, permanent spot for shampoo and soap instead of a hanging caddy. It's included in your scope and will be installed before the backer board is closed up.
Why isn't flooring included?
Flooring is scoped separately to keep this proposal focused and the price clear. If you'd like to add flooring, we can quote it as a change order at any time — before, during, or after the shower work.
What's your warranty on the work?
All labor is warrantied for 1 year from project completion. Materials and fixtures carry their manufacturer warranties on top of that. The hot mop waterproofing is the most critical piece — it's done by a specialist and fully warranted.
How does payment work?
Payment is split into four milestones: $1,000 at signing, 30% (less the $1,000) on Day 1, 40% at midpoint when rough work is done, and 30% at your final walkthrough. You're never paying ahead of the work.
