Overview

A Complete Bathroom Transformation

This proposal covers a full guest bathroom renovation at 650 Avenida Seville, transforming an outdated, compartmentalized layout into a clean, open, spa-caliber space. The project includes a complete gut demo, full plumbing relocation, a freestanding soaking tub, a walk-in shower with glass enclosure, a double vanity, a private toilet compartment, and the addition of a walk-in closet between the bath and bedroom.

All work will be performed by Timberline Build Co under Justin Sherman's GC license. Our primary tile and bathroom sub, Jose, will handle all tile installation. We will coordinate permits, inspections, and scheduling from start to finish.

Scope of Work

What's Included

The following scope is based on the architectural plans dated June 13, 2026, drawn by M.D. All work described below is included in the Investment Summary.

Demo & Demolition

  • Full gut demo — existing tile, walls, vanity, shower stall
  • Remove existing single-sink vanity and plumbing
  • Demo existing shower enclosure and surround
  • Remove interior partition walls per new plan layout
  • All debris haul and site cleanup

Framing, Drywall & Insulation

  • Frame new toilet compartment walls
  • Frame new walk-in closet (11'3" × 4'0") from bedroom space
  • Frame new walk-in shower walls and niche
  • Install cement board in all wet areas
  • Drywall throughout bath and closet
  • Insulate exterior closet wall

Shower

  • Hot mop shower pan (required for mud-set pan)
  • Full tile installation — shower walls, floor, niche (Jose)
  • Semi-frameless glass shower enclosure (supply & install)
  • Shower valve and trim installation (finish TBD — see Finish Budget)

Freestanding Tub

  • Freestanding soaking tub installation (tub supply — see Finish Budget)
  • All tub plumbing rough-in and connections
  • Tub faucet/filler installation (floor-mount supply lines)

Plumbing

  • Full plumbing relocation per new plan layout
  • Double vanity drain and supply rough-in (left wall)
  • Tub drain and floor supply rough-in
  • Toilet reconnect in new private compartment
  • All rough and finish plumbing included

Double Vanity Area

  • Bathroom floor tile installation (Jose)
  • Countertop fabrication and installation (stone cost — see Finish Budget)
  • Vanity cabinet installation (cabinet cost — see Finish Budget)
  • Faucet installation (faucet cost — see Finish Budget)

Walk-In Closet

  • Framing, drywall, and insulation (exterior wall)
  • Entry door from bathroom (2'6" × 6'8")
  • Entry door from bedroom (2'6" × 6'8")
  • Paint and trim throughout closet

Electrical

  • Relocate and update all electrical per new layout
  • GFCI outlets at vanity per code
  • Vanity lighting installation (fixture cost — see Finish Budget)
  • New vent fan installation

Doors, Trim & Paint

  • Toilet compartment door (supply, hang, and case)
  • Bathroom entry door (supply, hang, and case)
  • Closet door (supply, hang, and case)
  • Door hardware on all three doors
  • Baseboard — labor and material throughout bath and closet
  • Door casing — labor and material, both sides per door
  • Paint — entire bath and closet (walls, ceiling, trim)
