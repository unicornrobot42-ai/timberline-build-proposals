Project Overview

This contract covers three distinct scopes of work at the above property: (1) correction and completion of master bathroom and entry repairs left incomplete or improperly executed by a previous contractor, (2) new exterior and bedroom improvements, and (3) sliding door and window replacement. All work to be performed by Cuccia Construction, Inc. per the descriptions below.
