01 — Overview

What We're Building

This is a complete bathroom remodel at 434 Plaza Estival in San Clemente. We're gutting the existing bathroom and rebuilding it from scratch — new 36×42 tiled shower with a hot-mop pan, new tile throughout, a built-in double vanity with countertop and dual sinks, updated plumbing, frameless glass enclosure, and fresh finishes throughout.

Timberline manages all labor, waterproofing, plumbing, and finish work. Client purchases tile, vanity, countertop material, and fixtures within the allowances listed below — we handle pickup, delivery coordination, and full installation.

02 — Scope of Work

What's Included

  • Full demo — remove existing shower surround, floor tile, vanity, drywall, and backer board as needed
  • Hot mop shower pan — waterproof membrane for 36×42 shower base (required for all tiled shower pans)
  • Tile installation — 30 sq ft bathroom floor, full shower walls and floor (client purchases tile within allowance)
  • Plumbing — shower valve and trim, vanity supply lines, drain connections, toilet reset
  • Built-in double vanity installation — client purchases cabinet within allowance, we install
  • Countertop fabrication and installation — client purchases material within allowance
  • Dual sink and faucet installation — client purchases fixtures within allowance
  • Frameless shower glass enclosure — supply and install
  • Electrical — replace vent fan, switches, and outlets
  • Final clean and walk-through punch list

03 — Not Included

What's Not in This Proposal

The following are excluded from the project price. Where client is purchasing materials, Timberline will coordinate pickup and handle full installation — you just cover the cost of the materials themselves within the allowances shown below.

  • Tile material — floor and shower walls (client purchases; we install)
  • Double vanity cabinet (client purchases; we install)
  • Countertop material (client purchases; we fabricate and install)
  • Sink(s), faucets, and shower fixtures (client purchases; we install)
  • Permits — typically required in San Clemente; add $500–$1,500 depending on scope and city approval
  • Drywall repair or replacement
  • Paint — walls and ceiling
  • Any work outside the bathroom
  • Furniture moving or staging
  • Hidden damage discovered during demo — documented and priced via change order if encountered
