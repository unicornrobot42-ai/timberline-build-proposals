What We're Building

Scope of Work

01

Complete Bathroom Demo

We'll demolish the existing bathroom down to the framing. This means removing the old vanity, toilet, tub, flooring, and all fixtures. We'll salvage anything reusable and haul away everything else. Your space will be clean, empty, and ready for the new build.

02

Double Sink Vanity Installation

We're installing a beautiful built-in double sink vanity. This gives you plenty of counter space and dual sinks so mornings aren't a traffic jam. We'll hook up new plumbing, run new supply lines, and finish it with your choice of faucets (included in your fixture allowance).

03

New Bathtub & Tile Shower

Out goes the old tub. In comes a fresh, modern bathtub paired with a full tile shower surround. We'll tile the shower walls floor-to-ceiling with your selected tile (included in your tile allowance). New grout, new caulking, everything sealed properly so it lasts for years.

04

Tile Flooring Throughout

The entire bathroom floor gets fresh tile. We'll remove the old flooring, prep the subfloor, lay new tile with proper grout and sealing. Your tile selection is included in the flooring allowance—pick something that makes you smile every time you step in.

05

Plumbing & Fixtures

All new plumbing runs, new shut-off valves, and updated supply lines. We'll install your chosen bathroom fixtures—faucets, shower head, towel bars, toilet—everything modern and functional. All hookups are done to code.

Note: Fixture selections are included in your fixture allowance. You pick the finishes and style—we install them.
06

Glass Shower Enclosure

We're installing a frameless glass shower door. This gives your bathroom that open, airy, premium feel. The glass comes with proper sealing and hardware so it looks sharp and functions flawlessly.

Your dream bathroom is closer than you think.
