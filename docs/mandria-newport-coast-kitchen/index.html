Kitchen Remodel

Full gut & rebuild · Open to dining · Living room extension

Complete demolition and rebuild of the kitchen. The wall between the kitchen and dining area is removed, opening up the space. New cabinet installation throughout — kitchen uppers and lowers, a second row of upper cabinets, peninsula with 12-inch bar overhang, and an extension into the adjacent living room with additional cabinetry and bar top. Quartz countertops, backsplash labor, under-cabinet lighting (fixtures included), plumbing, and appliance installation are all included.

  • Full kitchen demo — cabinets, countertops, fixtures, hood; haul-away included
  • Wall removal — kitchen/dining opening; framing, drywall repair, paint on repaired surfaces
  • Cabinet installation — kitchen uppers + lowers, 2nd row upper cabs, peninsula, living room extension and bar top; labor only, client supplies cabinets
  • Quartz countertops — kitchen + dining extension; material, fabrication, and install included
  • Backsplash — tile installation labor; client supplies tile
  • Plumbing — sink rough-in + hook-up, refrigerator water line, garbage disposal install
  • Appliance install — range, dishwasher, microwave, refrigerator; labor only, client supplies appliances
  • Under-cabinet lighting — fixtures + wiring + install; all included
  • Patch and paint — demo and wall removal repair areas

Master Bathroom — Full Gut & Rebuild

Custom wet area · Freestanding tub + rain shower · Frameless glass · 125" vanity

A complete master bathroom transformation built for a Newport Coast home. The wet area — approximately 113×45 inches — is fully waterproofed, tiled floor to ceiling, and enclosed in a custom frameless glass enclosure. Freestanding tub sits inside the wet zone alongside a rain shower. Custom 125-inch double-sink vanity, linen pantry, and all-new plumbing throughout.

  • Decorative hardware + accessories — towel bars, TP holder, mirrors, hooks; client supplies items
  • Tile floor install — full master bath floor; labor only, client supplies tile
  • Hot mop — custom shower pan (113×45 wet area)
  • Full waterproofing — Schluter or equiv. system; all wet zone walls + floor
  • Cement board substrate — full wet zone structural prep
  • Tile labor — floor-to-ceiling, full wet zone including shower niche; client supplies tile
  • Shower plumbing — valve, drain, rain head, controls; client selects fixtures
  • Freestanding tub — set, plumb, connect filler; labor only, client supplies tub
  • Custom frameless glass enclosure — full 113×45 wet zone
  • Dual-sink vanity plumbing — rough-in + hook-up
  • Paint — non-wet areas; 2 coats, client selects color
  • Punch-out — final caulk, touch-ups, miscellaneous finish items
