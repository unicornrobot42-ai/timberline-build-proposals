Project Overview

What We're Doing

We'll be relocating an interior 5-foot wall approximately 24 inches to expand the home office and reduce the adjacent bedroom footprint. The scope includes full demo of the existing wall, new framing at the revised location, drywall on both sides, paint, and installation of closet doors on the office side.

This is a clean, efficient project. Typical turnaround is 3–5 days once materials are on site.

Scope of Work

What's Included

  • Mask off and site protection — floors, adjacent rooms, and furniture zones
  • Demo of existing 5-foot interior wall at current location
  • Framing of new wall at revised location (+24 inches into bedroom)
  • Drywall — hang, tape, mud, and texture to match existing on both sides of wall
  • Paint — both affected rooms, walls cut in and rolled to match
  • Closet door installation (labor only) — office side
  • Cleanup and haul-off of all demo debris
Exclusions

What's NOT Included

  • Closet doors — to be purchased and supplied by client
  • Door hardware — to be purchased and supplied by client
  • Flooring repair at previous wall footprint (available as add-on)
  • Load-bearing engineering or beam work (wall assumed non-structural)
  • Electrical or low-voltage work inside wall cavity
  • Furniture moving
