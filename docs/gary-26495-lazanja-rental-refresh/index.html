Project Overview

Rental-Ready Refresh

This proposal covers a comprehensive whole-home refresh at 26495 La Zanja St #11C to bring the unit to clean, modern, rental-ready condition. Scope includes new LVP flooring throughout, full kitchen update, two bathroom updates, new windows and exterior doors, interior doors, stair railing, closet doors, recessed lighting, fresh paint, and popcorn ceiling removal.

All work is performed by Timberline Build Co. and trusted subcontractors. No permits required for this scope. Work will be sequenced to minimize downtime and protect the property.
