Scope of Work

What's Included

Complete breakdown by phase. All labor and materials included unless noted as an allowance.

1

Demolition & Prep

Removal · Haul-Away · Substrate Prep
  • Full removal of existing cabinets, countertops, and backsplash
  • Disconnect and cap plumbing and electrical as needed for demo
  • Haul away all debris — job site kept clean daily
  • Subfloor and wall inspection prior to new install
2

Cabinetry & Countertops

Cabinets · Island · Quartz · Backsplash
  • New stock cabinet installation — material allowance + labor
  • Custom island with seating overhang — built and installed
  • Quartz countertops — material allowance + template + install
  • Full tile backsplash — material allowance + install
  • Soft-close hardware on all cabinet doors and drawers
3

Plumbing & Electrical

Sink · Dishwasher · Lighting · Outlets
  • Sink and faucet plumbing — rough and finish (fixture allowance)
  • Dishwasher hookup — client-supplied appliance
  • 6 recessed lights — wiring, cans, trim
  • Pendant light rough-in over island (fixture client-supplied)
  • Updated outlets — code-compliant GFCI at counter level
4

Flooring & Finishes

LVP Flooring · Paint · Final Walkthrough
  • LVP flooring — kitchen and adjacent nook (material allowance + install)
  • Touch-up paint on adjacent walls affected by demo
  • Final fixture installation, cabinet adjustment, and cleaning
  • Walkthrough with homeowner to confirm punch list is complete

Exclusions

What's NOT Included

The following are excluded unless added via written change order.

  • Appliance purchase — hook-up labor only (owner supplies appliances)
  • Pendant light fixture purchase — install only
  • Structural or layout changes beyond the scope above
  • Furniture removal, moving, or storage
  • Any damage discovered during demolition (subfloor rot, mold, outdated wiring, etc.) will be assessed on-site and addressed as needed — priced and approved before work proceeds
  • Any work not explicitly listed in this proposal
