01 — Overview

Project Overview

This proposal covers a comprehensive rental-prep remodel of the 7-bedroom, 3-bathroom home at 6963 San Palo Circle, Buena Park. Built in 1955, the property requires full-system updates to meet current California rental code, improve habitability, and position the home competitively in the OC rental market.

Scope includes all three bathrooms (full gut remodel), LVP flooring throughout common areas and stairs, whole-house drywall repair and paint, mold remediation, new windows and sliding doors, popcorn ceiling removal with updated lighting, electrical panel rebuild, exterior drain relocation, new trim throughout, and all required CA rental compliance items. An optional kitchen remodel is also included for client consideration.
