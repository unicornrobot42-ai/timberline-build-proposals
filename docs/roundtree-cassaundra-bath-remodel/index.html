Project Overview

This proposal covers a complete bathtub and surround replacement, including demolition of the existing tub and surround, rough and finish plumbing, drywall repair on the opposite wall, and installation of the new tub and wall finish. We've put together two options so you can choose the finish level that fits your goals and budget.
