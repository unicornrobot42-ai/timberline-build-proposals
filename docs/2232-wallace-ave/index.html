Here's What We're Doing

Scope of Work

01

Structural Assessment & Repair

We start by bringing in a licensed structural engineer to assess the floor plate damage and any other structural issues the water created. We'll replace the damaged sill plate with pressure-treated lumber, reinforce any compromised studs, and install proper flashing to prevent future water intrusion. This is the foundation of a lasting restoration.

💡 This is covered by insurance as structural damage. We'll work directly with your adjuster on this.
02

Demolition & Prep

We remove all flood-damaged drywall, flooring, and cabinetry. We clean the concrete slab, test it for moisture, and prepare it for new flooring. This phase is all about clearing away what's damaged and prepping the home for new materials. It's methodical and careful—we're protecting the parts that are fine while removing what needs to go.

03

Mold Assessment & Prevention

Water damage can hide mold issues. We conduct professional mold testing, and if we find anything, we treat it with antimicrobial spray. We also apply antimicrobial treatment to all exposed framing to prevent mold from establishing itself during construction. Your home will be safe and healthy when we're done.

04

Drywall, Paint & Interior Finishes

We replace drywall on all affected walls, tape and mud it smooth, and texture-match the existing patterns. Then we prime and paint to match your existing finishes. We handle wallpaper removal, door casings, and any trim work needed. This phase brings the home back to looking and feeling like home.

05

Flooring Throughout

We install luxury vinyl plank flooring across your entire home—1,200 square feet of new flooring. We seal the concrete slab first to prevent moisture issues, then install the LVP and new baseboards throughout. Your home will have consistent flooring and a fresh look everywhere.

06

Complete Kitchen Restoration

This is the centerpiece. We install new base and upper cabinets (shaker style), quartz countertops, a new sink and faucet, backsplash tile, and all new appliances (cooktop, wall oven, and range hood). We also handle all the plumbing work to reconnect your sink and dishwasher, and we reroute the gas line for your cooktop. Your kitchen will be fully functional and beautiful.

🔧 Gas line reroute requires a licensed gas fitter and city inspection. All covered in this estimate.
07

Electrical & Plumbing

All electrical outlets, switches, and wiring that was submerged gets replaced by a licensed electrician. We install new GFCI outlets in the kitchen and wet areas for safety. All work is inspected and certified. For plumbing, we reconnect everything and pressure-test the system. Every trade is licensed, every work is inspected.

08

Doors & Exterior Work

We replace the water-damaged exterior door and the living room sliding glass door. We also repair the stucco on the exterior where the water intrusion happened. Everything is sealed and painted to match your existing home.

Your home will be whole again.
