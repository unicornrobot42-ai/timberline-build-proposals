The Work

Scope of Work

01

Structural Assessment & Repair

We start with a licensed structural engineer to assess all damage. We replace the damaged floor plate with pressure-treated lumber, reinforce compromised studs, and install proper flashing to prevent future water intrusion. This is the foundation of a lasting restoration.

02

Demolition & Prep

We remove all flood-damaged drywall, flooring, and cabinetry. We clean and test the concrete slab for moisture, then prepare it for new flooring. Every piece of damaged material is hauled away. Your home becomes a clean slate, ready for restoration.

03

Mold Assessment & Prevention

We conduct professional mold testing. If we find anything, we treat it with antimicrobial spray. We also apply antimicrobial treatment to all exposed framing to prevent mold during construction. Your home will be safe and healthy when we're done.

04

Drywall, Paint & Interior Finishes

We replace drywall on all affected walls, tape and mud it smooth, and texture-match the existing patterns. Then we prime and paint. We handle door casings and trim work. Everything is finished to look like it was never damaged.

05

Flooring Throughout

We install new luxury vinyl plank flooring across your entire home—1,200 square feet. We seal the concrete slab first, then install the flooring and new baseboards throughout. Your home will have consistent, fresh flooring everywhere.

06

Complete Kitchen Restoration

New base and upper cabinets (shaker style), quartz countertops, new sink and faucet, backsplash tile, and all new appliances (cooktop, wall oven, range hood). All plumbing is reconnected and the gas line is rerouted. Your kitchen will be fully functional and beautiful.

07

Electrical & Plumbing

All electrical outlets and wiring that was submerged gets replaced by a licensed electrician. New GFCI outlets throughout. Gas line reroute by a licensed fitter. All work is inspected and certified. Every trade is licensed, every work is approved.

08

Doors & Exterior Work

We replace the water-damaged exterior door and the living room sliding glass door. We repair the stucco on the exterior where water intrusion happened. Everything is sealed and painted to match your existing home.

Your home will be whole again.
