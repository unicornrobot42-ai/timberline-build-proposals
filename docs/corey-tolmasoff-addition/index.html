The Vision

Project Overview

This project encompasses two distinct scopes at 21202 Foxtail, Mission Viejo: a new second-story bedroom addition paired with an expansion of an existing bedroom (built over the current 2nd-story balcony), and a full remodel of the existing guest bathroom.

Due to the structural nature of the addition, the project is phased: a design and engineering phase produces city-approved construction documents, followed by full construction once permits are issued. This protects both parties — no guesswork, no mid-build surprises.

Phase Two

Construction Scope

The full construction scope will be finalized and priced once architectural and engineering plans are approved. The following represents the anticipated scope of work based on the current project description:

Second Story Addition & Bedroom Expansion

  • Demo of existing balcony structure and related framing
  • New structural framing — floor system, walls, roof tie-in
  • New bedroom addition with closet and access
  • Bedroom expansion over converted balcony footprint
  • Exterior siding/stucco to match existing home
  • Roofing integration and weatherproofing
  • Windows and exterior doors
  • Insulation (wall, floor, ceiling) per Title 24
  • Drywall, tape, texture, paint (interior)
  • Electrical — new circuits, outlets, lighting, panel review
  • HVAC extension into new square footage
  • Flooring (allowance TBD pending selections)
  • Interior doors and hardware
  • Final inspections and punch list

Guest Bathroom Remodel

  • Demo — existing tile, fixtures, vanity, and surround
  • Hot mop if shower pan work is required
  • New tile — floor and shower walls (allowance TBD)
  • New vanity, countertop, and plumbing fixtures
  • Plumbing — rough and finish
  • Shower enclosure / frameless glass
  • Electrical — outlets, lighting, vent fan
  • Drywall, paint, and trim
  • Final punch list and inspections
