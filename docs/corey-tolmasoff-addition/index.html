The Vision

Project Overview

This project adds a new bedroom to the upper level of the home at 21202 Foxtail, Mission Viejo, and expands an existing bedroom by building out over the current second-story balcony. The result is additional square footage, improved functionality, and increased home value.

Due to the structural nature of this work, the project is best executed in two phases: a design and engineering phase to produce city-approved construction documents, followed by the full construction phase once permits are issued. This approach protects both the client and the contractor — no guesswork, no surprises mid-build.

Phase One

Plans, Engineering & Submittal

Phase 1 — Design & Permitting

What's Included

  • Complete site measurements and 3D scan of existing structure
  • Draft as-built site plan reflecting current conditions
  • Draft proposed addition floor plan
  • Full architectural sheet set for city submittal
  • Structural engineering calculations and stamped drawings
  • Construction document package prepared for city submittal

⚠ Phase 1 Does NOT Include:

Phase Two

Construction Scope

The full construction scope will be finalized and priced once architectural and engineering plans are approved. The following represents the anticipated scope of work based on the current project description:

  • Demo of existing balcony structure and related framing
  • New structural framing — floor system, walls, roof tie-in
  • New bedroom addition with closet and access
  • Bedroom expansion over converted balcony footprint
  • Exterior siding/stucco to match existing home
  • Roofing integration and weatherproofing
  • Windows and exterior doors
  • Insulation (wall, floor, ceiling) per Title 24
  • Drywall, tape, texture, paint (interior)
  • Electrical — new circuits, outlets, lighting, panel review
  • HVAC extension into new square footage
  • Flooring (allowance TBD)
  • Interior doors and hardware
  • Final inspections and punch list

* Bathroom addition, if requested, would be scoped and priced separately as an add-on item.
