Overview
A Complete Two-Bathroom Transformation

Timberline Build Co will perform a full remodel of both the master and guest bathrooms at 9 Mallard, Aliso Viejo. The master bath will receive an expanded walk-in shower with a full bench, widened shower opening, dual vanity refresh, updated plumbing, electrical, and fresh paint throughout. The guest bathroom will be converted from a tub surround to a modern walk-in shower with slider glass, new vanity, new window, updated plumbing, vent fan, and paint.


All finish materials (tile, stone, fixtures, vanity, sinks, faucets) are client-supplied. Timberline provides all labor, installation, demo, and structural work. See the Finish Material Guide section for budget ranges by item.

Scope of Work
What's Included

Master Bathroom

  • Full demo — existing shower/tub surround, vanity area, mirrors, med cabs
  • Framing modification — widen shower opening approx. 3" (pending in-wall inspection)
  • Hot mop shower pan
  • Tile installation — approx. 130 sq ft (shower walls, tub walls, bench) — client provides tile
  • Plumbing — tub rough-in, shower valve, dual sink supply and drain
  • Frameless glass enclosure — supplied and installed by Timberline
  • Electrical — new lighting, new vent fan per sketch
  • Drywall / cement board in wet areas
  • Vanity, cabinet, and mirror install — labor only, client-supplied fixtures
  • Countertop fabrication and installation — $900 labor; client supplies stone slab
  • Full bathroom paint
  • Final cleanup and haul-off

Guest Bathroom

  • Full demo — existing tub/shower surround, vanity, window
  • Hot mop walk-in shower pan
  • Tile installation — approx. 90 sq ft (3 walls, walk-in shower) — client provides tile
  • Plumbing — new shower valve, vanity sink supply and drain
  • Slider glass enclosure — supplied and installed by Timberline
  • Window replacement — labor to remove existing and install new window unit (client supplies window)
  • Electrical — replace vent fan, new lighting
  • Drywall repair — cement board wet area, window patch
  • Vanity install — labor only, client-supplied vanity and cabinet
  • Vanity light replacement — labor only, client supplies fixture
  • Mount mirror, towel bar, and TP holder — complimentary when client provides hardware
  • Full bathroom paint
  • Final cleanup and haul-off

What's NOT Included

  • All tile material (client-supplied — see Finish Material Guide below)
  • Tub, vanity/cabinets, sinks, faucets, shower fixtures, tub filler (client-supplied)
