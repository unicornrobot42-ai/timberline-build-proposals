Overview
A Complete
Two-Bathroom Transformation

Timberline Build Co will perform a full remodel of both the master and guest bathrooms at 9 Mallard. The master receives an expanded walk-in shower with full bench, frameless glass enclosure, updated dual vanity, countertop fabrication, new electrical, and fresh paint. The guest bath transforms from a tub surround to a clean, modern walk-in shower with slider glass, new vanity, new window, updated plumbing and vent fan.


All finish materials — tile, stone, fixtures, vanity, sinks, faucets — are client-supplied. Timberline handles all labor, installation, demo, and structural work. A finish material guide with OC market pricing is included below to make budgeting straightforward.
