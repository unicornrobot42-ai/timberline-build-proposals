Overview
A Complete Two-Bathroom Transformation

Timberline Build Co will fully remodel both the master and guest bathrooms at 9 Mallard. The master bath receives an expanded walk-in shower with full bench, frameless glass enclosure, updated dual vanity, countertop fabrication, new electrical, and fresh paint. The guest bath transforms from a dated tub surround into a clean, modern walk-in shower with slider glass, new vanity, window replacement, updated plumbing, and paint throughout.


Timberline handles all demolition, labor, installation, and structural work. Finish materials — tile, stone, fixtures, vanity, sinks — are client-selected, and Cynthia is responsible for material costs. We handle the rest.

🎨
We Partner You with a Designer
Every Timberline dual-bath project includes pairing with an interior designer who specializes in modern, cohesive spaces. Your designer will help you select tile, fixtures, vanity, and finishes that work beautifully together — so nothing feels mismatched, dated, or picked at random. The result is a bathroom that feels intentional and high-end, not assembled from a big box store. This is included in your project investment.

A note on "client-selected" materials: When we say an item is client-supplied, it means Cynthia selects the product and is responsible for its cost. Timberline will often coordinate the order or make the pickup — but the material cost flows to Cynthia directly, not through our labor price. This keeps your proposal honest and your budget accurate.

No drywall in wet areas. All tile substrates use cement board or Schluter-approved backer — no drywall behind tile. Drywall repair (as needed) covers only dry zones: vanity walls, ceilings, and window patches.
Permit Note: Bathroom remodels in OC cities may require permits depending on scope. Timberline can pull them at cost plus a coordination fee. Timeline may add 2–4 weeks if required.
