Bathroom Remodel Proposal

Thank you for choosing Timberline Build Co for your bathroom remodel. We're excited to transform your space into a beautiful, functional sanctuary. This proposal outlines the scope of work, timeline, and investment for your project.

Scope of Work

Complete Bathroom Remodel including:

  • Full demolition and debris removal
  • Moisture barrier installation (hot mop)
  • Ceramic tile flooring, walls, and shower surround
  • Plumbing rough-in and fixture installation
  • Custom vanity and cabinet installation
  • Quartz or granite countertops
  • Frameless glass shower enclosure
  • New toilet installation
  • Electrical wiring and lighting fixtures
  • Exhaust ventilation system
  • Drywall finishing and paint
  • Professional caulk and sealant work

Project Timeline

Week 1 Demolition, rough-in plumbing & electrical
Week 2 Tile installation, vanity, countertops
Week 3 Glass enclosure, fixtures, finishing
Final Walkthrough Quality check & project completion
