What We're Doing

Scope of Work

01

Demolition & Preparation

We'll carefully remove your existing vanity, bathtub, fixtures, flooring, and tile. Everything gets hauled away. Then we'll prep the surfaces—drywall repairs, substrate checking, and getting everything ready for the new installations. This is where we set the foundation for a quality bathroom.

Note: If we find structural issues or soft spots during demo, we'll notify you immediately and discuss options. Any unexpected damage discovery is handled through a change order agreement.
02

Waterproofing & Shower Pan

After tub installation, we'll cover the walls with cement board and use Red Guard waterproofing—this is the critical step that prevents water infiltration and mold growth down the line. We apply the moisture barrier, then install a quality shower pan. This is non-negotiable for longevity.

03

Flooring Installation

New tile or LVP flooring throughout, professionally installed. We'll match existing floor levels with adjacent rooms and ensure proper slope for drainage if applicable. Your allowance covers quality mid-range materials with professional labor and setting.

04

Tile Shower Walls

High-quality tile installation on all shower walls. We're talking precision cuts, proper mortar application, grout lines, and a sealed finish that'll look sharp for years. Your allowance covers tile material selection; we handle all the skilled labor.

05

Bathtub Installation & Set

Your new bathtub gets professionally installed, leveled, secured, and sealed. Plumbing connections are rough and finished with proper p-traps and overflow lines.

06

Double Sink Vanity & Backsplash

Custom built-in double sink vanity with custom quartz countertops and a 4" backsplash. Vanity installation includes securing to wall studs, installing the sink basin, countertop sealing, and undermount work. Your fixtures get connected and tested before we call it done.

07

Plumbing & Electrical

Rough plumbing includes water supply lines for dual sink faucets and tub/shower diverter. Vent and drain lines are properly installed with p-traps. Electrical covers GFCI outlets, lighting fixtures, and a bathroom vent fan with ductwork. Everything is code-compliant and inspected.

08

Glass Shower Enclosure

Frameless glass enclosure installed with proper sealing and hardware. We'll coordinate measurements, ordering, and installation to ensure a perfect fit and a premium look that catches light beautifully.

09

Final Touches & Cleanup

Final fixture installation, touch-up paint if needed, sealing tile grout, mirror installation, and final walk-through with you. We'll clean up completely—your bathroom is spotless and ready to use.
