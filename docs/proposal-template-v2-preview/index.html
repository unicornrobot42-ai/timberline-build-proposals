What's NOT Included

The following are excluded unless added via written change order.

  • Any work not explicitly listed in this proposal

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

The questions homeowners ask us most before signing.

What happens if you find damage once demo starts?
If we uncover something not visible before demo — subfloor damage, old plumbing, mold, etc. — we stop, show you what we found, and price it before any additional work happens. You approve it in writing first. Nothing gets added to your bill without your sign-off.
How do material allowances actually work?
Each allowance is a budget for a specific material (tile, fixtures, etc.) that's already built into your total price. You pick anything at or under that amount at no extra cost. If you choose something above the allowance, you only pay the difference. If you spend less, the difference is credited back to you.
What's your warranty on the work?
All labor is warrantied for 1 year from project completion. Materials and fixtures carry their manufacturer warranties on top of that.
Do I need to be home during the project?
Not for most of the day-to-day work, but we'll coordinate key moments with you — material selections, any decisions that come up, and the final walkthrough before we call it done.
How does payment work?
Payment is split into milestones tied to real progress — a deposit at signing, a midpoint payment once rough work is complete, and a final payment at your walkthrough and sign-off. You're never paying for work that hasn't happened yet.
