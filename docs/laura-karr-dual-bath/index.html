01 — Project Overview
Two Bathrooms. One Seamless Project.

This proposal covers a complete transformation of both bathrooms at 23062 Sonoita. Upstairs, the existing tub/shower combo becomes a fully tiled walk-in shower with frameless glass, a new 83" double vanity, recessed niche, and all-new electrical and plumbing. Downstairs, we're updating the vanity and adding bidet electrical — clean, efficient, done right.

All work performed by Timberline Build Co and our trusted trade partners, with Justin Sherman on-site throughout.
