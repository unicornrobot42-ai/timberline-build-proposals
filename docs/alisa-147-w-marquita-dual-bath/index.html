01 — Project Overview
Two Bathrooms, Done Right.

This proposal covers a complete renovation of both bathrooms at 147 W Marquita. The guest bathroom gets a full tub surround and floor tile refresh, new tub and plumbing, updated electrical, exterior-vented fan, vanity and countertop installation, and slider glass. The master bathroom converts the existing tub into a fully tiled walk-in shower, with a new pocket door, relocated electrical, double-sink vanity, and all-new plumbing throughout.

All work performed by Timberline Build Co and our trusted trade partners. Justin Sherman will be on-site throughout both bathrooms.
