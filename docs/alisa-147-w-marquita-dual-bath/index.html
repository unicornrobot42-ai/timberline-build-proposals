01 — Project Overview
Two Bathrooms. One Seamless Project.

This proposal covers a complete transformation of both bathrooms at 147 W Marquita. The guest bathroom gets a full update with a new tub surround, recessed niche, slider glass, and new vanity installation. The master bathroom converts from a tub/shower combo to a spacious walk-in shower with frameless glass, pocket door entry, and a new double-sink vanity.

Both spaces will be fully tiled, re-plumbed, and updated with new electrical and ventilation. By bundling both bathrooms into a single project, you receive coordinated scheduling and a 10% discount on the combined scope.
