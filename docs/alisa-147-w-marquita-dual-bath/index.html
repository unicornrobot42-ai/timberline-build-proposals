01 — Project Overview
Two Bathrooms. One Clean Project.

This proposal covers a complete renovation of both bathrooms at 147 W Marquita. The guest bathroom receives a full tub surround and floor tile, new vanity and countertop, slider glass, updated electrical, and a new exterior-vented fan. The master bathroom converts the existing tub into a fully tiled walk-in shower, adds a pocket door at the entry, relocates electrical for the new layout, and installs a double vanity with all-new plumbing.

Work performed by Timberline Build Co and our trusted trade partners, with Justin Sherman on-site throughout. Scheduling both bathrooms together saves time on setup, teardown, and trades — and we're passing those savings directly to you.
