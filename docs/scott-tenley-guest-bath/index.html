01 — Project Overview
A Fresh Start for Your Guest Bathroom

This project transforms the existing guest bathroom at your Tidemark home into a clean, elegant space featuring a walk-in tile shower, updated vanity, designer wallpaper, and refined wainscoting. The scope covers full demo of the existing tub, a new shower build, updated plumbing and electrical, and all finish work — leaving you with a bathroom that feels brand new.

All work is performed by Timberline Build Co and our trusted trade partners, with Justin Sherman on-site throughout the project.

02 — Scope of Work
What's Included
  • Demo and removal of existing bathtub and surround
  • Hot mop shower pan (waterproofing base for walk-in shower)
  • Full tile installation — floor-to-ceiling shower walls and floor (tile selection per allowance)
  • New plumbing rough-in and trim for walk-in shower and vanity hookup
  • Supply and set new pre-fab vanity (per allowance) with sink fixture hookup
  • Single glass panel with open entry opposite shower fixture
  • Built-in shower shampoo niche
  • Electrical: 3 new recessed can lights, replace existing vent fan, install 2 wall sconces (client-supplied fixtures)
  • Wainscoting — approximately 42" high, painted finish (materials and labor)
  • Wallpaper installation on all drywall surfaces above wainscot (material client-supplied)
  • Paint — wainscot only
  • Reset toilet (toilet not included)
  • Drywall patching as needed
03 — Exclusions
What's NOT Included
  • Existing floor — flooring to remain as-is
  • Sconce fixtures — client to supply; installation included
  • Wallpaper material — client to supply; installation included
  • Toilet — reset included, new toilet not included
  • Permits — available upon request; contact us to discuss
  • Painting of walls, ceiling, or surfaces other than wainscot
  • Furniture removal or storage
  • Any structural changes
