The Work

Scope of Work

01

Kitchen Demo & Prep

We're starting with a clean slate. We'll carefully remove your existing cabinetry, countertops, and appliances. Then we'll demo down to the studs where needed, haul everything away, and prepare the space for new construction. Your kitchen is going to look like a blank canvas — ready for something beautiful.

02

Cabinetry & Storage

Custom white shaker cabinets throughout — clean, timeless, and built to last. We're installing upper and lower cabinetry with soft-close hinges and interior organization systems. Your kitchen is going to have the storage you've always wanted. Plus, your island gets extended seating for the whole family.

03

Countertops, Backsplash & Sink

Beautiful quartz countertops with a waterfall edge on the island. Subway tile backsplash that extends to the ceiling. A new farmhouse sink with a premium faucet in aged brass. Everything's designed to work together and look intentional.

04

Appliances & Electrical

We'll install your new stainless steel appliances, including a gas range, refrigerator, dishwasher, and microwave. New electrical outlets throughout, dedicated circuits for major appliances, and recessed LED lighting on dimmers so you can set the mood however you want.

05

Flooring & Paint

New hardwood flooring that ties into your existing living room. Fresh paint in your selected color. Trim work and crown molding to finish everything off. Your kitchen is going to flow seamlessly into the rest of your home.
