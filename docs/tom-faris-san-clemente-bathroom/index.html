01 — Overview

What We're Building

This is a complete bathroom transformation at 309 Calle Escuela in San Clemente. We're gutting the existing tub/shower combo and converting the space into a fully rebuilt walk-in shower — with new tile, plumbing, vanity, frameless glass, and fresh finishes throughout.

For tile, vanity, cabinet, and fixtures, Tom purchases the materials — and we handle pickup, delivery coordination, and full installation. Timberline manages all labor, waterproofing, plumbing, electrical, and finish work from start to finish.

Design Inspiration  ·  Timberline Build Co

02 — Scope of Work

What's Included

  • Full demo — remove existing tub, shower surround, flooring, vanity, and drywall as needed
  • Hot mop shower pan — waterproof membrane installation (required for all walk-in showers)
  • Tile installation — walk-in shower walls, shower floor, and bathroom floor (full labor; Tom purchases tile)
  • Complete plumbing — new shower valve and trim, toilet reset, vanity supply and drain connections
  • Vanity and cabinet installation — Tom purchases, we pick up and install
  • Countertop fabrication and installation (Tom purchases material; we handle everything else)
  • Fixture installation — Tom purchases, we coordinate and install
  • Frameless shower glass enclosure — supply and install
  • Drywall — patch and replace as needed, moisture-resistant in wet areas
  • Electrical — replace outlets, switches, and covers throughout
  • New exhaust vent fan with duct
  • Paint — walls and ceiling
  • Final clean and walk-through
Optional Add-On

Window Replacement

Upgrade the existing bathroom window during the remodel. Estimated $900–$1,300 depending on size and style selection. Easy to add before we break ground — ask us about it.

03 — Not Included

What's Not in This Proposal

The following are excluded. Where Tom is purchasing materials, Timberline coordinates pickup and handles full installation — he just covers the cost of the materials themselves.

  • Tile material (Tom purchases — we pick up and install)
  • Vanity and cabinet (Tom purchases — we pick up and install)
  • Plumbing fixtures and hardware (Tom purchases — we install)
  • Countertop material (Tom purchases — we fabricate and install)
  • Any work outside the bathroom
  • Furniture moving
  • Hidden damage discovered during demo — documented and priced separately via change order if needed
