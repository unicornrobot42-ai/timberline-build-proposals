01 — Overview

What We're Building

This is a complete bathroom rebuild at 309 Calle Escuela in San Clemente. We're demolishing the existing tub/shower combo and converting the space into a full walk-in shower — new tile, new plumbing, new vanity, frameless glass, and fresh finishes throughout.

Tom is supplying tile, vanity, cabinet, and fixtures. Timberline handles all labor, waterproofing, installation, plumbing, electrical, and finish work. Our goal is to have this space completely transformed in 3–4 weeks.

Concept Render — Walk-In Shower Conversion  ·  Timberline Build Co

02 — Scope of Work

What's Included

  • Full demo — remove existing tub, shower surround, flooring, vanity, and drywall as needed
  • Hot mop shower pan — waterproof membrane installation (required for walk-in shower)
  • Tile installation — walk-in shower walls, shower floor, and bathroom floor (labor; tile supplied by client)
  • Complete plumbing — new shower valve and trim, toilet reset, vanity supply lines and drain connections
  • Countertop fabrication and installation (labor only; client-supplied material)
  • Frameless shower glass enclosure — supply and install
  • Drywall — patch and replace as needed, moisture-resistant in wet areas
  • Electrical — replace outlets, switches, and covers throughout
  • New exhaust vent fan with duct
  • Paint — walls and ceiling
  • Final clean and walk-through

03 — Exclusions

What's Not Included

The following are excluded from this proposal. Items marked as client-supplied will be installed by Timberline once delivered to the job site.

  • Tile material (client-supplied — labor included)
  • Vanity and cabinet (client-supplied — installation included)
  • Plumbing fixtures / hardware (client-supplied — installation included)
  • Countertop material (client-supplied — fabrication and installation labor included)
  • Permits — available upon request (San Clemente building dept; est. $500–$2,500)
  • Appliance purchase or haul-away
  • Furniture moving
  • Any work outside the bathroom
  • Hidden damage discovered during demo — addressed via change order if needed

07 — Warranty & Terms

Our Commitment
